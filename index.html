Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

Common varieties Nutritional information tags

55 to 65 days are needed from germination to harvest the root.

The usually deep purple roots of the beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable

In fact, Jim Halper once said this about beets, "Fact, bears eat beets. Bears, beets, Battlestar Galactica"
